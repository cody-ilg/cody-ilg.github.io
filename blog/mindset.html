Mindset

How can understanding neuroplasticity benefit you?

It can be disheartening to feel stuck in a bad habit you feel you have no control over. Realising we have control over our mental habits and can change them can restore agency to yourself!

How can you increase your neuroplasticity?

Doing small tasks often and repeating the same ones can strengthen the associated neroun connections. The more you do the stronger the pathways get and the easier it becomes.

Growth Mindset

A growth mindset is viewing aspects of your mind you wish to change as changable! Your minds abilities are not fixed, and even though you may feel its not where you want it to be, you can change that and improve.

Takeaways

Understanding neuroplasticity should empower you to take small steps to change yourself. By understanding how your mind can adapt, you are no longer shackled by self doubt or feelings of helplessness.
