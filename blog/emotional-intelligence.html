IQ and EQ. What are they?

IQ, or intellegence quotient, is a measure of your reasoning ability and intellegence. Using standardised tests, it gauges how good you are at problem solving, logic, rational deduction and reasoning.

EQ, or emotional quotient, is your ability to understand and manage your own emotions as well as communicate them to others. A person with high EQ will have strong empathy, adaptable behaviour and be able to resolve conflicts.
